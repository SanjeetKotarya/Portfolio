Behind the pixels

Here’s a quick peek into life beyond the screens. The same curiosity that drives my product work keeps me sketching, traveling, and collecting stories around IIT Madras and wherever design takes me.

I’m from Prayagraj, and creativity has shaped my life from the start—painting, sketching, woodworking, crafting, 3D art, animation, and video editing are all part of how I express ideas. Engineering Design at IIT Madras expanded that instinct, giving me a playground to explore visuals, design, and storytelling through both traditional and digital mediums. Living and learning across different environments keeps me curious, adaptable, and deeply collaborative.

You’ll find me skateboarding, experimenting with illustration styles inspired by cinema, or diving into sci-fi and horror through games and movies. These little adventures recharge me and help me translate complex concepts into simple, human experiences. Creativity isn’t just my skill—it’s how I understand the world and keep imagining what comes next.
