Vector Portraiture

In this collection of digital portrait illustrations, I explore the art of stylized vector portraiture. My work demonstrates a strong focus on form-shaping with light and shadow, the use of vibrant color palettes, and the challenge of capturing a subject's likeness in a clean, minimalist style.
