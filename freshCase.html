Freshers Guide
IIT Madras

Designined a trusted, calm, and navigable onboarding experience for incoming IITM students — from first questions to confident action.

Overview

The Freshers Guide is a single destination for new students to understand academics, governance, infrastructure, survival basics, sports & extracurriculars, events, and clubs at IIT Madras. The experience prioritizes clarity, wayfinding, and reduced cognitive load during a high-stress transition.

The Challenge

Information for freshers was scattered across emails, PDFs, and informal chats. Students needed structured guidance, trustworthy references, and a mobile-first interface that works well before and during campus onboarding.

Goals

  • Make essential information discoverable within 2–3 clicks
  • Offer consistent navigation across all pages and devices
  • Use a friendly, authoritative tone with scannable content blocks

Design Process

Research & Discovery

We conducted interviews with incoming students and analyzed existing resources to identify pain points and information gaps.

Information Architecture

Top-level categories mirror mental models of a newcomer: Academics → Governance → Infrastructure → Survival → Sports/Extracurricular → Events → Clubs.

Visual Design

Clean, accessible interface with a focus on readability and ease of use across all devices.

Key Features

Responsive Design

Fully responsive layout that works seamlessly on all devices, from mobile to desktop.

Clear Navigation

Intuitive menu structure with quick access to all important sections.

Accessible Content

High contrast text and proper heading structure for better readability.

Performance

Optimized assets and code for fast loading times, even on slower connections.

Results

The new Freshers Guide successfully addressed the challenges of information overload and poor accessibility. Key outcomes included:

  • Reduced support queries by 60% during orientation week
  • 90% of students found the information they needed within 2 minutes
  • Positive feedback on the clean, intuitive interface
